Kingdom of France

1444 — 1544

You are the ruler of France in the year 1444. A hundred years of destiny await.

How to Play:

  • Click End Year to advance time. Each year brings an event with choices that shape your kingdom.
  • Click an enemy province on the map to declare war and expand your realm.
  • Balance your Military, Gold, Stability, and Legitimacy — neglecting any one will bring ruin.
  • Win by controlling 10 of 14 provinces, or survive 100 years with 7 or more.
